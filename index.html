Thermal printing: Niimbot M2 is monochrome — it can only print black. Teal/colour pills will print as grey. Use White (holographic surface shows through) or Black (solid ink) for the pill on thermal labels.

Auto batch: Each download generates a unique BN-YYMMDD-XX number. Batch export gives each product its own number. Disable the toggle to use a fixed batch no. instead.
